Gulap Jamun

Ingrediants

  • 1 cup khoya
  • 1/4 cup mawa
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/2 tsp cardamom powder
  • Oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup milk

Preparation

  1. Mix khoya, mawa, sugar, cardamom powder, and milk in a bowl.
  2. Make small balls and fry them in hot oil until golden brown.
  3. In a separate pan, boil water and sugar to make syrup.
  4. Soak the fried balls in the syrup for 30 minutes.
  5. Serve warm or chilled.
